个人简介 / Personal Profile

我是詹泽凯，一名临床医学背景的研究者，目前在北京大学攻读精神病与精神卫生学硕士学位。 我的研究兴趣主要集中生物精神病学领域，专注于社交行为和孤独谱系障碍的表型， 期待利用新技术和新思路，完成精准诊断和个性化治疗。

我在中山大学完成了临床医学本科教育，奠定了坚实的医学基础和现代医学的思维模式。 随后，我选择在北京大学深造，希望通过跨学科的研究方法，为精神疾病的精准治疗贡献力量。

I am Zhan Zekai, a researcher with a clinical medicine background, currently pursuing a master's degree in Psychiatry and Mental Health at Peking University. My research interests mainly focus on the field of biological psychiatry, with a particular emphasis on social behavior and phenotypes of autism spectrum disorders. I aim to utilize new technologies and innovative approaches to achieve precise diagnosis and personalized treatment.

I completed my undergraduate education in Clinical Medicine at Sun Yat-sen University, establishing a solid medical foundation and modern medical thinking model. Subsequently, I chose to pursue further studies at Peking University, hoping to contribute to the precise treatment of mental illnesses through interdisciplinary research methods.
